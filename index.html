Our Story

Started in a garage, still run like one.

Ember Roastery began in 2019 when our founder started roasting one-kilo batches on a converted popcorn machine and handing bags to neighbours. Word spread faster than the beans could roast, and within a year we'd moved into a small storefront on the corner of 5th and Pine.

We're still small on purpose. Every batch is tasted before it's bagged, every label is printed the day the beans are packed, and every order is roasted to be sold — never to sit on a warehouse shelf. That's the whole philosophy: freshness over scale.

Sourced

Direct from the farm

We work with three growing partners in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra, paying well above commodity price for lots we choose ourselves.

Roasted

In batches of 12kg

Small drum, close attention. Every batch is logged, tasted, and adjusted before the next one goes in — no set-and-forget profiles.

Delivered

Within two days of roasting

Coffee peaks a few days after roasting. We bag, seal, and ship fast so what lands on your counter is still close to its best.

The Roast

Light enough to taste the farm, dark enough to love the cup.

Our roaster reads every batch by sound, smell, and colour before she reads it by a chart. Lighter roasts stay bright and floral to show off origin character; our house blend goes a little darker for the people who want body and a bit of bittersweet cocoa in the finish.

Curious what a roast level actually tastes like before you buy? The roast meter on each product on our menu shows exactly how far we took it, from light to dark.
