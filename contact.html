Find Us

We're on the corner of 5th and Pine.

Ember Roastery is a small-batch coffee roaster and café located in the heart of downtown. Come by to see the roaster in action, grab a bag of beans, or enjoy a cup of coffee brewed from our latest batch.

We're open Monday through Friday from 7am to 5pm, and Saturday from 8am to 3pm. Closed on Sundays.
