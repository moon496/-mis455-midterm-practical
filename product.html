Brewing at Home

One grind size doesn't fit every method.

A pour-over wants a grind about the texture of table salt and a total brew time near three minutes. A French press wants it coarser, closer to breadcrumbs, so the mesh filter doesn't clog and the grounds don't turn bitter from over-extraction.

If you're not sure where to start, ask us to grind your bag for the method you're using — every order form on the contact page has a note field for exactly that.
